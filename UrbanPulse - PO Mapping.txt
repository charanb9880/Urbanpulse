Project Title:
UrbanPulseAI – Smart Traffic Intelligence and Decision Support System
Project Type:
Mini Project
Project Domain:
Artificial Intelligence | Machine Learning 
Problem Statement
Rapid urbanization, population growth, and the increasing number of vehicles have made traffic congestion and road incidents major challenges in modern cities. Accidents, flooding, potholes, road blockages, and other unexpected events frequently disrupt traffic flow, resulting in increased travel time, fuel consumption, environmental pollution, and delays in emergency response. Existing transportation systems mainly focus on individual functionalities such as traffic prediction, navigation, or incident reporting, leading to fragmented solutions that lack coordinated traffic management. In addition, the absence of intelligent incident verification, emergency-aware routing, and integrated decision-support mechanisms limits the reliability of citizen-generated reports and reduces the ability of transportation authorities to respond effectively. Therefore, there is a need for an intelligent transportation framework that integrates traffic prediction, AI-assisted incident verification, optimized and emergency-aware routing, consequence analysis, and authority-oriented decision support to enable efficient, reliable, and proactive urban traffic management.
Project 
Objectives
	•	To predict future traffic conditions using Graph Neural Networks by capturing both spatial dependencies and temporal variations within urban road networks.
	•	To provide intelligent route recommendations that assist commuters in selecting efficient travel paths based on predicted traffic conditions.
	•	To enable citizens to report traffic-related incidents and automatically verify the authenticity of uploaded incident images using AI-based image analysis techniques.
	•	To support traffic authorities with real-time traffic intelligence and decision-support mechanisms for effective congestion and incident management.
	•	To contribute towards safer, smarter, and more sustainable urban transportation through proactive and data-driven traffic management.

Project 
Outcomes

	•	Developed an integrated intelligent transportation platform that combines traffic prediction, incident management, route optimization, and decision support within a unified framework.
	•	Improved the reliability of citizen-generated incident reports through Artificial Intelligence-based image verification, reducing false and misleading submissions.
	•	Enabled proactive traffic management by predicting congestion patterns and generating optimized as well as emergency-aware route recommendations.
	•	Empowered transportation authorities with a centralized dashboard for real-time traffic monitoring, consequence analysis, and data-driven decision-making.
	•	Enhanced citizen engagement by providing an interactive incident reporting platform and AI-assisted guidance for traffic-related services.
	•	Established a scalable and modular framework that supports future integration with real-time traffic data and smart city infrastructure, contributing to safer and more efficient urban mobility.
